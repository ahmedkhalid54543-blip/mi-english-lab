# 场景图 Prompt 包（Mi English）

统一风格建议（先作为全局前缀）：

- `clean premium educational app illustration, xiaomi orange accent, soft daylight, modern retail/business environment, clear character actions, balanced composition, high detail, no text, no watermark, no logo, 16:9`

建议参数：

- Midjourney: `--ar 16:9 --stylize 120 --quality 1`
- SDXL/Flux: CFG 4-6，steps 28-40

---

## 1) 门店服务与需求确认（Store Service & Needs）

**Prompt**

在现代智能手机门店内，店员微笑接待进店顾客，双方站在展示台旁进行需求沟通。画面包含门店入口、产品体验区、收银台、陈列架、柔和橙蓝配色灯光。店员一手指向演示机，一手做引导手势；顾客认真聆听。构图清爽，高级感、品牌化、移动端卡片可裁切。clean premium educational app illustration, xiaomi orange accent, soft daylight, modern retail environment, clear character actions, no text, no logo, no watermark.

**Negative Prompt**

low quality, cluttered background, crowded faces, distorted hands, aggressive expressions, dark scene, heavy shadows, text overlay, watermark, logo

---

## 2) 产品介绍与对比推荐（Product Introduction）

**Prompt**

门店产品讲解场景：店员在展示台前做两款手机并排对比，屏幕上显示相机与续航对比图标（无文字）。顾客站在侧面上手试拍，桌面有配件但不杂乱。画面强调“讲解 + 体验 + 对比”的动作关系，整体明亮、简洁、专业。premium UI illustration, retail tech store, side-by-side product comparison, subtle gradients, orange and light blue accents, no text.

**Negative Prompt**

messy shelf, too many products, unrealistic perspective, cartoon exaggeration, dark color cast, text labels, brand logos, watermark

---

## 3) 库存协同与调拨沟通（Inventory & Allocation）

**Prompt**

店内后场与前场联动场景：前场店员用平板查询库存，后场同事在货架区核对箱贴，屏幕上出现抽象库存状态图形（无文字）。氛围体现“缺货沟通 + 调拨确认 + ETA同步”，人物表情冷静专业。空间有层次但简洁，强调执行感。clean operational illustration, retail backroom + frontdesk collaboration, soft natural light, premium brand aesthetic, no text.

**Negative Prompt**

chaotic warehouse, dirty floor, dramatic lighting, panic expressions, random text, labels, watermark, logo, low detail

---

## 4) 渠道周会动销复盘（Channel Weekly Review）

**Prompt**

会议室周会场景：4-6位业务成员围坐讨论，屏幕展示抽象趋势图和柱状图（无文字），一位主持人站立讲解并指向图表。人物动作体现“复盘、提问、分配动作”，桌面简洁有笔记本和平板。整体是国际化企业风格，干净、现代、可信赖。premium business illustration, modern meeting room, collaborative review meeting, soft contrast, orange accent details, no text.

**Negative Prompt**

board full of readable text, chaotic composition, exaggerated poses, dark moody lighting, low resolution, watermark, logo

---

## 5) 跨文化协同与共识推进（Cross-cultural Alignment）

**Prompt**

跨国视频会议场景：大屏上有多地区参会者窗口（不显示文字），现场两位同事在讨论时间线卡片与任务节点图形（无文字）。氛围友好、理性、协作，突出“先对齐目标再讨论限制”的感觉。配色偏浅灰蓝，点缀小米橙，画面高级、克制、国际化。clean cross-cultural business illustration, global team collaboration, timeline icons, premium app visual style, no text, no logo.

**Negative Prompt**

flags everywhere, political symbols, harsh contrast, crowded faces, random typography, watermark, logo, noisy background
