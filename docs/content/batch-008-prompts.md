> 共 50 张 | 风格：可爱扁平插画，幽默夸张，辅助记忆
> 英文单词本身出现在图中（词根/词缀用橙色高亮），其余文字全部中文
> 输出格式：PNG 1024×1024 | 存放路径：`assets/memory/{文件名}`
>
> **通用前缀**：
> `Cute flat illustration style, soft rounded shapes, warm pastel colors with orange accents, clean simple background, exaggerated humorous scene. Apart from the English word/phrase itself, all other text must be in Chinese. No extra English labels, captions, or speech bubbles.`
